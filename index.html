Software engineering

After graduating, students will have extensive theoretical and practical knowledge, and have the ability to organize, deploy and be creative in work in the software field.

Career prospects

Vietnam's technology market is growing rapidly, attracting the attention of many large companies in the industry. According to the Draft National Strategy, Vietnam will focus on four types of digital technology companies: digital technology startups, digital solutions, core technology development, and digital products and services. . According to some predictions, Vietnam will have more than 1 million companies with nearly 1.2 million information technology workers by 2026.

For example, currently, the average salary of programmers in Vietnam is somewhere around 8 - 25 million VND per month. For web programmers, the front-end salary is from 7 - 12 million VND, and the back-end salary is from 12 - 17 million VND. Management positions can receive higher salaries from 26 - 60 million VND, and will likely continue to increase in the future. In addition, programmers with Blockchain skills can receive salaries 2 to 3 times higher than normal due to large recruitment demand because the workforce is not large, only accounting for 6 - 10% of the market.

Due to the strong development of modern technology, the Software Engineering industry in Vietnam is becoming an attractive "prey" for many young people - considered an advanced profession in the field of information and communication technology. pine. Along with the huge job opportunities in this industry, it is also difficult to find qualified human resources.
